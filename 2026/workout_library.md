# WORKOUT LIBRARY

Standard workouts used by the training system.

The AI coach should prioritize these workouts when generating training sessions.

---

# VO2MAX WORKOUTS

## 5 × 1000

1000m at 5K pace  
Recovery: 2 min jog

Purpose:
VO₂max development and race pace tolerance.

---

## 6 × 800

800m slightly faster than 5K pace  
Recovery: 90s jog

Purpose:
oxygen uptake and fatigue resistance.

---

## 4 × 1200

1200m at 5K pace  
Recovery: 2 min jog

Purpose:
extended VO₂ stimulus.

---

# THRESHOLD WORKOUTS

## 3 × 2000

2000m at threshold pace  
Recovery: 2 min jog

Purpose:
lactate clearance.

---

## 20 min tempo

Continuous run at threshold effort.

Purpose:
aerobic power.

---

## 2 × 3 km tempo

3 km at threshold pace  
Recovery: 2–3 min jog

Purpose:
extended threshold tolerance.

---

# RACE PACE WORKOUTS

## 5 × 1000 @ 5K pace

Classic race preparation workout.

---

## 3 × 1600 @ 10K pace

Improves sustained race rhythm.

---

## 3 × 5 km @ HM pace

Half marathon simulation workout.

---

# SPEED / ECONOMY

## 10 × 200

Fast but relaxed.

Recovery: 200m jog.

Purpose:
neuromuscular speed.

---

## 8 × 400 @ 3K pace

Recovery: 90s jog.

Purpose:
speed endurance.

---

## Strides

6–8 × 100m fast relaxed accelerations.

Purpose:
running economy.

---

# LONG RUNS

## Progressive long run

18–24 km.

First half easy  
Second half faster.

Purpose:
fatigue resistance.

---

## Long run with HM pace

Example:

16 km easy + 5 km HM pace.

Purpose:
late race durability.

---

## Fast finish long run

20 km easy  
last 4 km steady.

Purpose:
finish strength.

---

# FARTLEK WORKOUTS

## Classic fartlek

10 × (1 min fast / 1 min easy)

Purpose:
speed endurance and aerobic power.

---

## Pyramid fartlek

1–2–3–4–3–2–1 minutes fast  
equal recovery.

Purpose:
mixed intensity stimulus.

---

## Long fartlek

5 × (4 min fast / 2 min easy)

Purpose:
threshold stimulus without rigid pacing.

---

# HILL WORK

## Hill sprints

8–10 × 10 sec steep hill

Full recovery.

Purpose:
power and neuromuscular strength.

---

## Hill repeats

6 × 60–90 sec uphill

Jog back recovery.

Purpose:
strength endurance.

---

## Rolling hills run

Steady run on hilly terrain.

Purpose:
strength + aerobic durability.

---

# RACE SIMULATION

## 3 × 3000

3000m at 10K pace  
Recovery: 2 min jog

Purpose:
race rhythm and pacing.

---

## Broken 5K

2000 + 1600 + 1200 + 800

Recovery: 2 min jog.

Purpose:
race fatigue simulation.
